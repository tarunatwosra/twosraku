# Spiritual Module
Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

Spiritual is the centralized spiritual development module of Twosraku.

The module supports schools in recording, monitoring, and evaluating students' participation in spiritual development activities.

Spiritual focuses on participation, consistency, personal growth, and institutional reporting rather than assessing religious belief.

Operational records remain objective and based on observable activities.

---

# Philosophy

Participation builds habits.

Habits shape character.

Character supports leadership.

Spiritual development is a continuous process.

Every recorded activity should support student growth.

---

# Primary Objectives

Manage spiritual activities.

Record student participation.

Monitor participation trends.

Provide spiritual development summaries.

Integrate with Character Points and Statistics.

Support reporting.

# Final Principle

Spiritual is not an attendance module for religious activities.

Spiritual is the spiritual development module of Twosraku.

Its purpose is to support schools in fostering consistent participation, positive habits, and holistic student development through structured spiritual programs while maintaining objective and auditable records.
