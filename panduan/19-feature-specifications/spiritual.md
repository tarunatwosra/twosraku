# Spiritual Module — Compact
Version: 1.0

**Purpose:** Spiritual is Twosraku's centralized spiritual development module, supporting schools in recording, monitoring, and evaluating students' participation in spiritual development activities. It focuses on participation, consistency, personal growth, and institutional reporting rather than assessing religious belief — records remain objective and based on observable activities.

**Philosophy:** participation builds habits; habits shape character; character supports leadership. Spiritual development is a continuous process; every recorded activity should support student growth.

**Primary Objectives:** manage spiritual activities; record student participation; monitor participation trends; provide development summaries; integrate with Character Points and Statistics; support reporting.

**Scope:** Spiritual Dashboard, Activities, Participation, Attendance, Reflection, Achievements, Reports, Statistics, Settings.

**Integrated Modules:** Student Registry, Attendance, Character Points, Statistics, Dashboard, Reports, Notifications, Future Parent Portal.

**Navigation:** Spiritual → Dashboard, Activities, Participation, Reflections, Achievements, Statistics, Reports, Settings.

### Dashboard
**Purpose:** overview of spiritual development activities.
**Widgets:** Total Activities, Participation Rate, Today's Activities, Upcoming Activities, Outstanding Participants, Recent Reflections, Monthly Summary, Quick Actions.
**Quick Actions:** Create Activity, Record Participation, Add Reflection, Generate Report, Search Student.

### Spiritual Activities
**Purpose:** manage spiritual development programs.
**Properties:** Activity Name, Category, Description, Date, Time, Location, Supervisor, Academic Year, Semester, Status, Notes.
**Example Activities:** Morning Prayer, Congregational Prayer, Scripture Reading, Religious Study, Religious Celebration, Character Reflection, Community Service, Charity Activity, Leadership Reflection, School Spiritual Program. Schools may customize activity types.
**Categories:** Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Semester, Annual, Special Event, Community Service, Custom.

### Participation
**Purpose:** record student participation.
**Record:** Student, Activity, Date, Attendance Status, Participation Level, Notes, Recorded By.
**Attendance Status:** Present, Absent, Excused, Late, Cancelled.
**Participation Level:** Excellent, Good, Satisfactory, Needs Improvement, Observed — descriptive and configurable.

### Reflections
**Purpose:** record observations related to student participation and development.
**Properties:** Student, Activity, Reflection Date, Observation, Recommendation, Recorded By.
**Business Rules:** optional; become part of the student's historical record; only authorized users may edit reflections.

### Achievements
**Purpose:** record recognition related to spiritual development.
**Types:** Outstanding Participation, Leadership, Community Service, Consistency, Positive Influence, Special Recognition. Become part of the student's profile.

### Search & Filters
- **Search by:** Student, Activity, Category, Date, Supervisor, Academic Year, Semester.
- **Filters:** Academic Year, Semester, Activity, Class, Major, Participation Status, Participation Level, Date Range.

### Reports
Participation Report, Activity Report, Class/Major/Student Summary, Annual Report, Participation Trend, Reflection Summary.

### Statistics
Participation Rate, Activity Frequency, Most Active Classes/Students, Monthly/Annual Trend, Activity Distribution. Generated by the Statistics Module.

### Character Integration
Spiritual activities may contribute to character development, but character records remain managed by the Character Points Module — no character score is modified directly within Spiritual.

### Notifications
Activity Scheduled, Participation Recorded, Upcoming Activity, Reflection Added, Report Generated.

### Settings
Configure: Activity Categories, Participation Levels, Attendance Status, Reflection Templates, Report Templates, Default Supervisor.

### Permissions
| Role | Access |
|---|---|
| Administrator | Full Access |
| Principal | View All |
| Vice Principal | Manage Activities |
| Teacher | Manage Participation |
| Homeroom Teacher | View Assigned Students |
| Staff | Limited Access |
| Students (future) | View Own Participation |

### Audit Log
Tracks: Activity Created/Updated, Participation Recorded, Reflection Added, Achievement Recorded, Report Generated, Settings Updated. Immutable.

### Performance Requirements
| Action | Target |
|---|---|
| Dashboard | < 500 ms |
| Participation Entry | < 1 second |
| Statistics | < 2 seconds |
| Participation Records Supported | 100,000+ |

Server-side processing required.

### Accessibility
Keyboard Navigation, Screen Reader Support, High Contrast, Visible Focus, Responsive Layout, WCAG AA.

### Security
Role-based Access, Permission Validation, Audit Logging, Soft Delete, Immutable History, Encrypted Communication.

### Future Enhancements
Parent Portal, Student Self Reflection, Digital Certificates, QR Attendance, Activity Registration, Volunteer Hours, Community Service Tracking, AI Reflection Summary, Calendar Integration, Mobile Companion.

### Definition of Done
Complete when: activities manageable; participation recorded accurately; historical records traceable; statistics integrate with the Statistics Module; reports reproducible; permissions enforced; performance targets achieved; accessibility standards satisfied; follows the Design System.

### Final Principle
Spiritual is not an attendance module for religious activities — it is the spiritual development module of Twosraku. Its purpose is to support schools in fostering consistent participation, positive habits, and holistic student development through structured spiritual programs while maintaining objective and auditable records.
